Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

NASTAVNYK Analytics is an internal analytics dashboard used to understand website traffic and social media performance for NASTAVNYK educational services.

Information We Process

When a connected account authorizes analytics access, we may process:

  • basic public profile information such as display name, username, avatar, and account ID;
  • account-level statistics such as follower count, video count, likes count, or similar metrics;
  • public content performance data such as video URLs, publish dates, views, likes, comments, and shares;
  • website analytics from Google Analytics 4, such as sessions, users, landing pages, campaigns, and events.

How We Use Data

We use this data only to prepare internal reports, monitor marketing performance, improve educational content, and assign operational tasks to the NASTAVNYK team.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by the services required to run this dashboard, including Google Sheets, Google Analytics, Meta, TikTok, hosting providers, and secure local automation tools.

Data Retention

Analytics data is kept only as long as it is useful for reporting, compliance, and business operations. Access tokens and credentials are stored outside the public repository.

Your Choices

A connected account can revoke access in the relevant platform settings, including TikTok, Meta, or Google account permissions. After access is revoked, future collection from that platform stops.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact NASTAVNYK at nastavproject@gmail.com.
